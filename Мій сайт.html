About Me

Creative Student | Digital Art & Drawing

Komar Yuliia

I am interested in digital drawing and creating images using a computer. Instead of traditional tools such as a pencil or paints, I use a laptop mouse to draw directly on the screen. I enjoy experimenting with shapes, lines, and colors while creating digital illustrations. That is why I want to inspire you as well, so you understand that anyone can draw, and there is always an opportunity for a new start and creative development.

Inkscape & Digital Drawing News

Learning Inkscape for Digital Art

Inkscape has become one of the most popular free programs for vector drawing. Artists use it to create illustrations, logos, posters and digital paintings. The official Inkscape website now provides tutorials, tracing lessons and creative design examples for beginners.

Popular YouTube Channel About Inkscape

Davies Media Design is one of the best-known YouTube channels for learning Inkscape and digital illustration. The channel publishes tutorials about vector graphics, creative effects, logo creation and modern digital drawing techniques.
